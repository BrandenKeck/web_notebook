Welcome to My Notebook

The purpose of this website is to document thoughts, projects, and fun ideas for the purpose of sharing with others online. It is my goal to keep postings informal and to avoid pet peeves of mine (such as reposting the same images found in every tutorial on a given topic, etc.). Anyone who happens to stumble upon this page can feel free to share and reuse this content as they please. I will be practicing typesetting all postings in Latex using the wonderful tools over at Overleaf and will make the PDFs available for distribution. All code used on this site (as well as the PDFs) will be made available through Github as well!
